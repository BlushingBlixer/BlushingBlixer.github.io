Gifts of Old Faith

Gifts of Old Faith is a short film animation proposal I developed which centers around its character design in juxtaposition to is visual world design. It is about a found family and their playful relations with the occult, I've explored and developed their characters, backgrounds and visual design surrounding the concept.

Baba Yaga

Baba Yaga and Her Companion Scratches by BlushingBlixer on Sketchfab

Baba Yaga is a 3D model I developed from concept to final. Inspired by russian folklore, she was designed for 3D animation and features a full facial and body rig. Shape language and visual storytelling is an important part of her character.

Fan Works

Working in a production team sometimes means adhearing to a predetermined art style and direction. My collection of fan works showcase my ability to achieve this whilist still creating original art and characters.

Bunny Game Character

Muffin is a 2D Bunny character created as a video game asset. He is designed as a layered pupet so that he is able to be easily manipulated by programmers. The game model features several points of procedural animation.

ABOUT

Hello! My name is Eden Takenaka and I am a character artist and concept designer from Queensland Australia. I am passionate about art and design and have worked on a number of small scale productions.

I am an experienced artist with 2D proficiencies in Adobe Photoshop, After Effects and Procreate. And 3D proficiencies in Maya, Substance 3D Painter and Blender.
